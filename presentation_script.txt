# 🚦 PreClear AI — Presentation Script
# Tone: Interactive, Humorous, Engaging
# Estimated Duration: 2:45 minutes

[START]

(Presenter stands in front, gesturing to the Landing Page - http://localhost:5173/)

PRESENTER:
"Good morning everyone! Quick question to start... Raise your hand if you’ve ever sat at a red light, at 2 AM, when there wasn’t a single other car in sight?"

(PAUSE - wait for hands)

PRESENTER:
"Exactly. We’ve all been there. And how did that make you feel? Like a productive member of society? Or like the universe was personally testing your patience?"

(PAUSE - chuckle)

PRESENTER:
"Well, today, we’re ending that. Welcome to PreClear AI — the brain your city's traffic signals never had."

(SCROLL DOWN the landing page - show the animations)

PRESENTER:
"Now, another obvious one. If an ambulance is behind you, and there's 5 miles of bumper-to-bumper traffic, what do you do? A) Magically levitate? B) Delete the traffic? Or C) Watch helplessly as precious minutes tick away?"

(PAUSE)

PRESENTER:
"The answer is C... until now. Let’s move to the Live Heatmap."

(NAVIGATE to http://localhost:5173/traffic-data)

PRESENTER:
"Look at this. This isn't just a pretty map. Those colors—Green, Yellow, Red—are updating every 5 seconds. We’re currently feeding data from a dataset of 15,000 real-world records."

(Wait for a marker to change color - Point it out!)

PRESENTER:
"See that? Hebbal Flyover just went from Red to Green. Why? Because our AI predicted the surge and cleared it. We don't just react to traffic; we *delete* it before it becomes your problem."

(NAVIGATE to http://localhost:5173/route-planner)

PRESENTER:
"But let's talk about *your* commute. Let's plan a route from Hebbal to Silk Board. Now, normally, Google Maps would say 'Good luck, you'll be home by Tuesday.'"

(SELECT Hebbal and Silk Board - CLICK "Find Optimal Route")

PRESENTER:
"But PreClear uses an A* routing algorithm integrated with our LightGBM and LSTM models. It’s analyzing the congestion *segments* in real-time. See those badges? We’re choosing the path with the least resistance."

(PAUSE - dramatic effect)

PRESENTER:
"And finally, the 'God Mode'. The Ambulance button."

(CLICK the 🚑 Ambulance button)

PRESENTER:
"When an emergency is detected, it’s not just about a siren. It’s about communication. PreClear forces a 'Green Corridor' along this exact path, clearing the road so that help arrives *zero* seconds late."

(CLOSING)

PRESENTER:
"So, to wrap up: Do we want to keep sitting at lights waiting for a sign? Or do we want a city that actually *thinks* for us? I think the answer is pretty obvious."

PRESENTER:
"I'm from the PreClear Team, and we’re here to make sure you never have to levitate your car again. Any questions?"

[END]
